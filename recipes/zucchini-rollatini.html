Zucchini Rollatini

Description

These zucchini rollatini are ribbons of zucchini, filled with ricotta, mozzarella, and herbs, and baked in marinara sauce, a bit like lasagna roll-ups, but without the pasta.

Ingridients

  • 2 (12 ounce) zucchinis, sliced lengthwise into 18 (1/8-inch-thick) planks
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt, divided
  • 1 cup whole milk ricotta cheese, patted dry
  • large egg, lightly beaten
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh basil, plus more for garnish
  • 2 teaspoons chopped fresh oregano
  • 2 teaspoons grated lemon zest
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 clove garlic, grated
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded whole milk mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese, divided
  • cooking spray
  • 2 cups jarred marinara sauce (such as Rao’s®), divided
  1. Step 1: Gather all ingredients. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C), with racks in the top and lower thirds.
  2. Step 2: Toss zucchini with oil and 2 tsp salt on 2 large rimmed baking sheets; spread in a single layer. Bake until tender, about 10 min, rotating pans halfway through.
  3. Step 3: Remove from oven and cool zucchini for 5 min. Lower oven to 375°F (190°C).
  4. Step 4: In a bowl, mix ricotta, egg, basil, oregano, lemon zest, pepper, garlic, 1 cup mozzarella, 1/4 cup Parmesan, and 1 tsp salt until combined.
  5. Step 5: Coat a 13x9" baking dish with spray. Spread 1 cup marinara on bottom. Pat zucchini dry. Place 1 tbsp+1 tsp ricotta mix at one end of slice, roll up, and set seam-down in dish. Repeat.
  6. Step 6: Pour remaining 1 cup marinara over center of rolls (leave ends exposed). Sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup mozzarella and 1/4 cup Parmesan.
  7. Step 7: Bake at 375°F until bubbling and browned, about 25 min. Let rest 10 min. Garnish with basil.
