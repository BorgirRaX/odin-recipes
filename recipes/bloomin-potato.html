Bloomin’ Potato

Description

These bloomin' potatoes, savory and salty, are tender with a crisp edge. The garlic-onion seasoning is great, the potatoes are delicious, and the dipping sauce is tasty.

Ingridients

  • 4 (6 to 8 ounce) russet or Yukon gold potatoes
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted

Sauce

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon prepared horseradish
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  1. Step 1: Wash and scrub potatoes thoroughly until clean.
  2. Step 2: Using a sharp knife, slice potatoes crosswise into 1/8-inch thick cuts, stopping 1/2-inch from the bottom so the potato stays attached at the base. Rotate and repeat lengthwise, also making 1/8-inch thick cuts without slicing all the way through.
  3. Step 3: Place cut potatoes in a 2-quart square baking dish. Cover with vented plastic wrap and microwave on high for 5 minutes.
  4. Step 4: In a small bowl, mix garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper. Brush potatoes with melted butter, then sprinkle with the seasoning mixture.
  5. Step 5: Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking pan with aluminum foil, place potatoes on the pan, and bake until browned and crispy, 15–20 minutes.
  6. Step 6: In a medium bowl, mix mayonnaise, sour cream, ketchup, horseradish, vinegar, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper until smooth.
  7. Step 7: Serve potatoes hot with dipping sauce on the side.
